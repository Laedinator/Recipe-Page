How to make Green Tea

Green tea is the healthiest beverage on the planet. It is loaded with antioxidants and nutrients that have powerful effects of the body. These include improved brain function, fat loss, a lower risk of cancer and many other impressive benefits.

ingredients

  • Green Tea (Teabag or dried leaves)
  • Boiled Water
  • Ginger (Optional)
  • Lime (Optional)
  • Sugar or Honey (Optional)

preparation

  1. Boil water
  2. Optional: Cut some Ginger and Lime (wash both under hot water before!)
  3. Pour just a little boiled water over the Green Tea (either the Teabagbags or the leaves) and let it sit for 5 to 8 seconds, then pour the water out
  4. Pour the boiled water in the cup with the Tea (and put the optional ingredients in it aswell!)
  5. Let it sit for a while, then take the Teabagbags or leaves and the optional ingredients out (the longer they sit, the more taste!)
  6. ENJOY
